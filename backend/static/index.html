How Carbon Score is Calculated

Understanding your daily environmental standings.

Your daily activities generate CO2 emissions, measured in kilograms (kg CO2). The score represents your efficiency in comparison to target benchmarks:

Score 100 (Eco Guardian) ≤ 2.0 kg CO2 / day
Score 50 (Eco Steady) 16.0 kg CO2 / day
Score 0 (Eco Critical) ≥ 30.0 kg CO2 / day

A linear formula interpolates your score between 2.0 kg and 30.0 kg. Any footprint above 30.0 kg results in a score of 0.

Daily Benchmarks:

  • Target daily budget: 6.0 kg CO2 (corresponds to a score of 85+)
  • Global daily average: 15.0 kg CO2 (corresponds to a score of 53)

🌱 Action Tip: Try reducing travel distance, using solar energy, or swapping meals to vegetarian or vegan to boost your score!
